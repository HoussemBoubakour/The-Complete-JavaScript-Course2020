Game Rules

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as he whishes, each result get added to his ROUND (CURRENT) score.
  • BUT, if the player rolls a '1' with one of the dice, all his ROUND score gets lost, after that it's the next player's turn.
  • The player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls two double '6' in a row. And it's the next player's turn.
  • He can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • You must enter the winning score at the beginning of the game and the first player to reach that number on GLOBAL score wins the game.
